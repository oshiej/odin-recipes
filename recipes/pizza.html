Pizza Recipe

Description

A pizza that contains pepperoni, cheese and sauce.

Ingredients

  • Pizza base
  • Cheese
  • Pizza sauce
  • Pepperoni

Method

  1. Preheat oven to 180C
  2. Spread Pizza sauce over pizza base
  3. Spread cheese over sauce
  4. Place pepperoni over pizza evenly
  5. Place pizza on a lined baking tray and put in the oven for 15-20 minutes
  6. Remove from oven and let rest for 2 minutes
  7. Cut pizza and enjoy!
