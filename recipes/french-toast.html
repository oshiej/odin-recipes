French Toast

Description

French Toast involves bread coated in egg and cooked on a pan. Toppings can include anything you like, but this recipe will use biscoff as its only topping

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Egg
  • Milk
  • Biscoff

Method

  1. Mix egg and milk in bowl
  2. Coat bread in egg mixture
  3. Cook coated bread in pan on medium heat
  4. Remove from pan, spread biscoff on top and enjoy!
